The Falling ladder

A 16 feet ladder is leaning against a wall on flat ground. The wall is to the left of the ladder. The base of the ladder starts to slide away from the wall. When the base has slid to 8 feet from the wall, it is moving horizontally at the rate of 4 feet/second.

How fast is the ladder’s top sliding down the wall when the base is 8 feet from the wall?
